Subscription Payments

Once a subscription is activated, payments are generally non-refundable. Refunds are considered only in limited circumstances described below.

Exceptional Cases

Refund requests may be considered only in cases of technical failure preventing access to the service, duplicate payments, failed activations, or other verified billing errors.

Approved refunds are issued through the original payment method where possible.
